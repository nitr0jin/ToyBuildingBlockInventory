Our Story

The Block is dedicated to providing a more affordable way for children and adults alike to get their fill on toy building blocks through individual pieces or sets of blocks. Each building block is filled with love and a determination to keep our customers happy and to ensure that every moment playing with an item from The Block becomes a key memory in their life.

Values

Our values consist of the Three C's:

  • Nurture Creativity
  • Boost Confidence
  • Create Memories
